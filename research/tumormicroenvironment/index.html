Analysis of the Tumor Microenvironment

Nov 15, 2024
Image credit: Microbiome harbored within tumors

We aim to explore the immune and microbiome microenvironments of tumors, investigating their composition and function, as well as their impact on tumor development. By integrating multi-omics data, we seek to reveal how the tumor microenvironment influences cancer progression.

Laboratory of Shanghai Center for Systems Biomedicine

Our research aims to utilize machine learning and artificial intelligence, combined with systems biology, to comprehensively characterize the genotypes, molecular phenotypes, and clinical phenotypes within the tumor microenvironment. Our goal is to explore the intricate relationships among these three aspects in order to contribute to the advancement of precision oncology.
